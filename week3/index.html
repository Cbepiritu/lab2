THE WITCHER

A witcher is a fictional character from the book series "The Witcher" by Andrzej Sapkowski. Witchers are monster hunters who undergo extensive physical and magical training, as well as being infused with potions and mutagens that enhance their abilities. They are hired to deal with supernatural threats such as monsters and curses and are known for their expertise in tracking and combat. The series has been adapted into a popular video game franchise and a television series.

Nilfgaard

Nilfgaard is the name of an empire whose people are referred to as Nilfgaardians. They are a culturally diverse group with different ethnic backgrounds, languages, religions, and customs. Nilfgaardians are known for their military discipline, advanced technology, and sophisticated bureaucracy. They are often portrayed as pragmatic and results-oriented, valuing efficiency over tradition and honor. However, individual Nilfgaardians have their own unique personalities, motivations, and moral compasses.
